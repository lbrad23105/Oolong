OOLONG




OOLONG is an extremely simple layer of design for the Mobile Web.
Combining Material Design elements and JavaScript, OOLONG creates an immersive experience without clogging your Mobile Network.
OOLONG allows for complete customization while maintaining a framework. This is the best way to create a web app, having structure and then building on top of that.
